Working Together to End Hunger

This initiative is to enhance education with adequate nutrition. While it is necessary to have underprivileged children attend school, it is also just as important to have them eat regular meals in a day. This initiative also aims to sensitize the teachers and families on the need of proper nutrition. The influence of nutrition on education is often ignored, thereby deeming it necessary to highlight the relevance.
